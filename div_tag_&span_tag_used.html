A paraphrase is a restatement of the meaning of a text or passage using other words. The term itself is derived via paraphrasis, from Ancient Greek παράφρασις 'additional manner of expression'. The act of paraphrasing is also called paraphrasis.

QuillBot integrates directly into Chrome and Microsoft Word. Now you don’t have to switch windows every time you want to rephrase a sentence, paragraph, or article
